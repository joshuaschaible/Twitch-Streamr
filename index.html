What is Twitch Streamr?

Crafted for teams and communities, Twitch Streamr allows you automatically reterieve and display members from a specific Twitch team on your website (similar to Speed Runs Live). You also have the option to manually specify which channels you wanted to retrieve rather than pulling from a specific team.
